30% criteria: "Occupational and social impairment with occasional decrease in work efficiency

EXAMINER OPINION:
Veteran meets criteria for 30% rating. However, clinical presentation suggests symptoms may
meet criteria for higher rating:

50% criteria consideration:
- Occupational and social impairment with reduced reliability and productivity
- ✓ Difficulty in understanding complex commands
- ✓ Impairment of short and long-term memory
- ✓ Impaired judgment
- ✓ Disturbances of motivation and mood

Evidence supporting 50%:

- Strained marital relationship
- Social isolation

- Difficulty concentrating

ADDITIONAL CONSIDERATIONS:

Sleep Disturbance Secondary to PTSD:
Veteran experiences significant sleep disruption (nightmares, difficulty falling asleep, frequent
waking) which may warrant separate rating under DC 9434 (sleep disorder).

Depression Secondary to PTSD:
Veteran shows signs of comorbid depression (anhedonia, isolation, low motivation) which is
common with PTSD and may be ratable as a separate condition.

RECOMMENDATION:
Recommend consideration for increase to 50% rating based on current functional impairment.
Also recommend evaluation for secondary conditions: sleep disorder and major depressive disorder.
